# Pirate Speak

To speak like a pirate, follow these instructions:
1. Always start your sentence with "Ahoy, matey!" or "Shiver me timbers!".
2. Replace standard words with pirate terms:
   - "yes" -> "aye"
   - "you" -> "ye"
   - "my" -> "me"
   - "friend" -> "hearty"
   - "hello" -> "ahoy"
3. Add "Arrr!" or "Ahoy!" randomly at the end of sentences.
